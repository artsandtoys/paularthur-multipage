about

An Afro / Indo Caribbean composer and creative technologist born and raised in the global city of Miami, Paul is an audio/visual artist whose work blends the sonic aesthetics of different genres of music with immersive visual narratives. Emerging from Miami’s vibrant club circuit, he became a regular fixture in iconic Miami Beach venues like Mansion Nightclub and the beloved Louis Bar, has held rotations stretching from the US to Switzerland, and his music has been featured on editorial and official playlists ranging from YouTube Music to Pandora. Among the first DJs to establish a residency in the now iconic Wynwood Art District, contributing to the cultural foundation of a scene that would soon draw thousands and thousands each season and re-develop the Miami landscape! Drawing inspiration from multidisciplinary creators like Pharrell, Tycho, and Deadmau5, Paul has focused on building music that bridges live performance, club energy, and multimedia expression.
